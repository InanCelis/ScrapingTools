Description

Located in the exclusive residential community of La Mina, Puerto Ángel.

Wake up every day to the sound of the waves and sweeping views of the Pacific from this stunning two-bedroom, two-bathroom oceanfront home in the gated community of La Mina. With 2,368 Ft2 of beautifully designed living space, this brand-new residence blends modern architecture with tropical charm, creating the ultimate retreat by the sea.

The home is thoughtfully laid out over two levels, with spacious open-concept interiors that flow seamlessly onto expansive terraces. Both bedrooms and the main living area open to large outdoor spaces, designed to maximize the breathtaking ocean views. A palapa-covered terrace provides a shaded lounge area, while the private pool and outdoor shower invite you to embrace the coastal lifestyle year-round.

The principal bedroom is a sanctuary with dramatic views of the ocean, complemented by a luxurious en suite bathroom where even the shower overlooks the sea. Throughout the home, high-quality finishes such as aluminum doors and windows with a warm wood finish add style and durability.

This oceanfront home is being sold fully furnished and move-in ready, complete with a gas stove, oven, microwave, and refrigerator. A dedicated laundry area is included, and while there is no private parking on-site, the property is set within a secure, gated community for peace of mind.

Designed for those who value comfort, style, and connection to nature, this residence offers the perfect blend of indoor and outdoor living. Whether you are looking for a full-time coastal residence, a vacation home, or a turnkey rental opportunity, this property in La Mina delivers an exceptional lifestyle by the ocean.

*The property is listed at $19,780,000 MXN, with pricing shown in USD for marketing purposes.
