    Site and location

    • A fantastic location

      This beautiful development is situated a 5-minute drive away from Ashbourne, where you'll find a range of independent shops, a Sainsbury's supermarket, a Leisure Centre, and Ashbourne Park. The Peak District is also located within 4.3 miles, perfect if you enjoy the outdoors. 

      Derby, Burton-upon-Trent, and Stoke-on-Trent are within easy reach via the A52 by car. There are also local buses near by which connect you to Matlock, Nottingham and Burton. Your nearest railway is just 12-13 miles away in Uttoxeter or Derby.

      Local schools consist of OFSTED rated 'Good' schools within catchment area, including Queen Elizabeth's Grammar School (QEGS) and St Oswald's CofE Academy. 

      This development is also just a 5-minute drive from essential services, including St Oswald's Community Hospital, Ashbourne Fire Station, and dental practices.
