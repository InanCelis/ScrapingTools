KEY FEATURES

The Trump International Hotel & Tower Dubai brings together three iconic worlds in one address — a five-star hotel, a members-only club, and private residences, each with its own state-of-the-art amenities.

Trump International Hotel & Tower

A five-star luxury hotel, joining the legacy of Trump organization most iconic hotels. Featuring fine dining, world-class entertainment, and true luxury at every turn.

The Trump, Members-Only Club

The Trump Members-Only Club brings together wellness, business, and the timeless atmosphere of a classic private club. Access is limited to members only through an annual fee, with residents receiving priority access.

The Trump Residences

Featuring 1, 2, & 3-bedroom apartments and 4-bedroom penthouses, the residences dominate the skyline — with every unit offering a view. Residents also enjoy exclusive access to the highest outdoor pool in the world.

AMENITIES OF THE TRUMP RESIDENCES

The following amenities are reserved exclusively for residents. Residents can also apply for membership to the Trump Members-Only Club to access its iconic spaces and experience the full lifestyle.
