      Site and location

      • Essentials on your doorstep

        The Spires is ideally placed for residents to commute to  Chesterfield,  Worksop and  Sheffield.

        Located close to great amenities including shops, pubs and restaurants. There’s plenty to do as a family with days out nearby such as Bolsover Castle or Matlock Farm Park.

        OFSTED-rated ‘Good’ schools nearby including Inkersall Spencer Academy, Staveley Junior School, Outwood Academy and Whittington Green.

        Efficiently designed homes

        You could save up to £979 a year on your bills with a new energy-efficient home.

        Selected homes  at The Spires feature brand new technologies, meaning your new home is up to 65% more energy-efficient than a second hand home.

        Offering an A/B EPC rating, your new home could also include improved ventilation, Solar Panels, highly efficient insulation and more!

        Sustainable living

        We're focused on creating a sustainable home for everyone, whether they're human or wildlife.

        We're creating over 14 acres of open space and hedgehog highways for our prickly friends.

        There will be over 500 new trees planted, plus bee-friendly planting and over 50 bat and bird boxes placed around the development.
